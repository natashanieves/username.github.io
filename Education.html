Virtual Reality: Education

Url Virtual Reality as a Tool in the Education
Site Author Sandra Dutra Piovesan
Abstract Piovesan et.al state “education can be seen as a discovery, exploration and observation process, besides the eternal construction of the knowledge” With the addition of virtual reality into school curriculum it can evolve the future of education. During this study they propose that the new educational software has a purpose to help students complete their work and have a more interesting view on course material. It is also proposed to make learning more simple for students. The educational software provided pages with multiple choices where students needed to test their theoretical knowledge if they answered the questions provided correctly. Results showed that students who used the Vr educational software were more engaged and showed improvement.
  
Url The Effect of Using Virtual Reality in 6th Grade Science Course the Cell Topic on Student's Academic Achievements and Attitudes towards the Course
Site Author Serhan Sarioglu and Sonmez Girgin
Abstract This article explains a study conducted by Sarioglu and Girgin to test whether the use of Virtual reality in the classroom would improve academic success and boost attitudes in class. As the technology in the world is advancing everyday, students could use different types of technologies to overcome certain difficulties they may be facing. Many younger students have a short attention span and struggle to sit through class and pay attention. Sarioglu and Girgin believe that Considering the promises and potential of virtual reality technology, it can be considered as an application that can bring a new breath to science education. To conduct their research, they 100 6th grade students and performed a series of experiments and then answered an achievement test that was examined. The study revealed that students using the virtual reality technology had a positive effect on their academic achievement.
