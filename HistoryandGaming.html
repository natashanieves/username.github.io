Virtual Reality: History and Gaming

Url History of VR - Timeline of Events and Tech Development
Site Author Dom Barnard
Abstract Barnard's article explains how Virtual Reality has developed over the years. He starts off by discussing some of the earlier VR technologies, such as the Sensorama and The Telesphere Mask. He also briefly mentions the use of Vr in the Air Force flight simulators. After discussing the first attempts at virtual reality, he discusses the first VR headsets that were released and what software was used to create them including the popular Oculus Rift that was released in 2010, and bought by Facebook’s CEO Mark Zuckerburg. The last Vr product he discusses is the Apple Vision Pro which is a mixed-reality headset that was released on June 5, 2023.
  
Url How Virtual Reality is Revolutionizing the Video Gaming Industry
Site Author Shreya Mattoo
Abstract Shreya Mattoo's article also dives into little bit of detail on the history of virtual reality, and explains more about virtual reality itself and the different types of gaming systems. She describes how laptops, gaming consoles, and smartphones use virtual reality and the hardware that is used to create the virtual environment. VR hardware combines several tangible and intangible components wired to suspend a user in a 3D gamer’s world. The different color panels that are used in the headsets, allow for users to see a better color display. Lastly, the article discusses the benefits of virtual reality gaming as well as how it is transforming the gaming industry.
